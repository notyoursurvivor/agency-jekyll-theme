Mission

Not Your Survivor's (NYS) mission is to create transparency around an issue that is rarely talked about in our society due to a heavy barrier of shame. NYS approaches family sexual abuse (FSA) from the perspective that, as a society, we can’t begin to address or heal what we don’t talk about. NYS seeks to erase the taboo around FSA, all the while growing a supportive, understanding community for those who have experienced FSA and their allies.

Equity

We believe that everyone should have access to the healing guidance, techniques, and care that they need.

Community

We strive to ensure that no one has to go through the pain of healing from family sexual abuse alone.

Courage

We wholly embrace the courage it takes to claim ourselves, our potential, and the lives we're meant to lead.
