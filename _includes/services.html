Mission

The vision of NYS is to create transparency around an issue that is rarely talked about in our society due to a heavy barrier of shame. NYS approaches family sexual abuse (FSA) from the perspective that, as a society, we can’t begin to address or heal what we don’t talk about.NYS seeks to erase the taboo around FSA, all the while growing a supportive, understanding community for those who have experienced FSA and their allies.
